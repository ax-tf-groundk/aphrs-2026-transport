Information · 이용 안내

Usage Guidelines

Eligibility & Boarding

  • This shuttle is free of charge for registered APHRS 2026 participants. Please carry your conference registration confirmation or badge.
  • A QR boarding pass is required. Please have it ready on your phone or printed before boarding.
  • During peak hours the shuttle may be at full capacity. If so, please wait for the next scheduled service — it will arrive within 30 minutes.
  • Real-time tracking is not available. Shuttle operates on a continuous circuit — please wait at the designated stop.
  • Schedule is subject to change. Last shuttle departs at 21:00. Please check on-site signage for any updates.
