⚠️
eSIM Device Compatibility — Check Before You Order

Not all smartphones support eSIM. Before placing an eSIM order, please verify:

  • Your device model is eSIM-capable (iPhone XS or later, Samsung Galaxy S20+, Google Pixel 3+, etc.)
  • Your device is unlocked or supports Korean carrier eSIM profiles
  • Your carrier-at-home has not restricted eSIM provisioning

If unsure, we recommend the Physical SIM option — compatible with any unlocked handset.
