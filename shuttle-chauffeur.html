Information · 이용 안내

Usage Guidelines

Booking & Pricing

  • Advance booking is required — walk-in reservations are not accepted.
  • Please book at least 24 hours in advance of your required pickup time.
  • Fares are TBD and will be confirmed at the time of booking. Payment details will be provided upon reservation.
  • Cancellations must be made at least 12 hours before the scheduled pickup. Late cancellations may incur a fee.
  • Your driver's contact details will be included in the e-voucher sent upon confirmation.
  • Meet-and-greet service is available at Gimhae Airport international arrivals upon request.
