How to Book KTX

01

KORAIL Official Website

Visit letskorail.com to search schedules and reserve your seat up to 1 month in advance.

02

Korail Talk App

Download the Korail Talk app (iOS / Android) for mobile booking, QR tickets and real-time seat availability.

03

Station Ticket Window

Purchase at any major KTX station counter. Credit cards and cash accepted. English-speaking staff available at Seoul Station.

ℹ️
APHRS Shuttle Connection from Busan Station

After arriving at Busan Station by KTX, APHRS shuttle buses are available to BEXCO Convention Center and official conference hotels. Journey time is approximately 40 minutes. Please proceed to the designated APHRS pick-up zone at the main exit.
